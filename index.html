Adult cardiac arrest — ACLS
AHA Circular Algorithm · 2025
AHA 2025
1
Start CPR. BVM + O₂. Attach monitor/defibrillator.
2
Shockable (VF/pVT)? YES → Shock biphasic 120–200 J, resume CPR 2 min. NO (PEA/asystole) → CPR 2 min + IV/IO + epi ASAP.
3
Epinephrine 1 mg IV/IO q3–5 min for all rhythms.
4
Amiodarone 300 mg IV/IO (2nd dose 150 mg) OR Lidocaine 1–1.5 mg/kg for refractory VF/pVT.
5
Advanced airway: ETT or supraglottic. Waveform capnography. 1 breath q6 sec with continuous compressions.
6
Hs & Ts: Hypovolemia · Hypoxia · H⁺ acidosis · Hypo/hyperkalemia · Hypothermia · Tension pneumo · Tamponade · Toxins · Thrombosis PE/MI
Push ≥2 in at 100–120/min, full recoil, minimize interruptions, 30:2 ratio without airway. Change compressor q2 min.
Post-cardiac arrest care (ROSC)
AHA PCAC Algorithm · 2025
AHA 2025
A
Airway: assess + advanced airway. Confirm with waveform capnography.
O
Oxygenation: 100% FiO₂ until SpO₂ measurable → target 90–98% (PaO₂ 60–105). PCO₂ 35–45 mmHg.
H
Hemodynamics: MAP ≥65 mmHg. Vasopressors + fluids as needed.
D
Diagnostics: 12-lead ECG. Consider CT head/chest/abdomen. POCUS if available.
T
Temperature: not following commands → 32°C–37.5°C ASAP. EEG for seizure evaluation.
Avoid hypoglycemia <70 and hyperglycemia >180. Prognostication ≥72h from ROSC.
Pediatric cardiac arrest — PALS
AHA/AAP · 2025
AHA 2025
1
Start CPR. BVM + O₂. Attach monitor/defibrillator.
2
VF/pVT: 1st shock 2 J/kg → 2nd 4 J/kg → subsequent ≥4 J/kg (max 10 J/kg or adult dose).
3
Epi 0.01 mg/kg IV/IO (max 1 mg) q3–5 min. Amiodarone 5 mg/kg IV/IO (max 300 mg) for refractory VF/pVT.
4
CPR ratios: 15:2 with 2 rescuers pre-puberty · 30:2 post-puberty or 1 rescuer. With advanced airway: 1 breath q2–3 sec.
5
Reversible causes + hypoglycemia (add to standard Hs & Ts).
Neonatal resuscitation
AHA/AAP · 2025
AHA/AAP 2025
1
Term? Good tone? Breathing/crying? → YES to all → skin-to-skin, routine care.
2
Warm, dry, stimulate, position, clear airway. Pulse ox + cardiac monitor.
3
Apnea or HR <100: PPV. Labored breathing/cyanosis → O₂, consider CPAP.
4
HR <100 after PPV: ventilation corrective steps (MR SOPA). Consider intubation or LMA.
5
HR <60: intubate + chest compressions 3:1 + 100% O₂ + UVC or IO + epi q3–5 min.
6
SpO₂ targets: 2 min 65–70% · 3 min 70–75% · 4 min 75–80% · 5 min 80–85% · 10 min 85–95%
LVAD patient — unresponsive
AHA · 2025
AHA 2025
1
Assist ventilation if needed. Assess perfusion (skin color/temp, cap refill, MAP >50 via Doppler, PETCO₂ >20 via ETT).
2
Adequate perfusion → DO NOT start chest compressions. Assess and treat non-LVAD causes.
3
No perfusion → chest compressions. Assess LVAD: listen for hum, check alarms, driveline + power connected?
LVAD patients may have NO palpable pulse — absence of pulse ≠ cardiac arrest. PETCO₂ via supraglottic airway gives falsely elevated reading. Contact VAD center and transport.
STEMI — LERN referral center protocol
LERN · ACCF/AHA 2013
LERN Official
1
ECG within 10 min. PCI center within 60 min? YES → transfer + pre-activate cath lab. NO → thrombolytics + transfer. Door-in-door-out ≤30 min.
2
Aspirin 325 mg · NTG · Cardiac monitor · O₂ if SpO₂ <94% · IV NS
3
TNKase (STEMI box in med room, single bolus): <60 kg=30 mg · 60–69=35 mg · 70–79=40 mg · 80–89=45 mg · ≥90=50 mg. Do NOT shake — swirl only. Informed consent required.
4
Clopidogrel: 300 mg (≤75 yrs) or 75 mg (>75 yrs). UFH: 60 units/kg bolus (max 4,000) + 12 units/kg/hr.
Absolute CI: prior ICH · intracranial neoplasm · ischemic stroke <3 months · aortic dissection · active bleeding · head trauma <3 months · uncontrolled HTN.

LERN Jun 2026

LERN + ABA

Sepsis — hour-1 bundle
SSC 2021 · UGH 640.ED.1028
1
Screen every patient. SIRS ≥2: Temp >100.5 or <96.8, HR >90, RR >20, SBP <90 or MAP <65, AMS, Lactate >2, WBC abnormal.
2
Lactate within 30 min of arrival. Redraw at 4h if initial >2.0.
3
Blood cultures × 2 within 30 min — before antibiotics.
4
Broad-spectrum abx within 60 min. UGH: Vancomycin 20 mg/kg (max 2g) IVPB × 1.
5
LR 30 mL/kg for SBP <90 or MAP <65 or lactate ≥4 — complete within 3h.
6
Norepinephrine if hypotension persists. Target MAP ≥65.
